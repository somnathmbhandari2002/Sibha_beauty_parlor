Who We Are

About SIBHA Foundation

SIBHA Foundation (Snehashraya IBHA Foundation) is committed to empowering individuals and organizations by promoting safety, dignity, and inclusion. With a strong focus on Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH), we work closely with organizations to build awareness, ensure compliance, and create respectful workplace cultures.

We also extend our efforts to child protection through POCSO awareness and community-based initiatives. We bring a practical, compliance-driven approach backed by real-world experience in POSH implementation, IC processes, and organizational training.

"A trusted partner in workplace safety, compliance, and social impact initiatives."

Our Mission

✓ Promote safe workplaces through POSH awareness and compliance

✓ Foster inclusive environments through gender sensitization

✓ Support organizations in ethical and legal practices

✓ Drive awareness on child protection under POCSO

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual experiences safety, dignity, and equal opportunity in all aspects of life and work.

Our Offerings

Our Services

Our services are designed to support organizations at every stage, from awareness and training to compliance and policy implementation.

POSH Awareness & Training

Educate employees and management on the Prevention of Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act, ensuring a safe, respectful, and legally compliant environment.

  • Understanding sexual harassment forms
  • Legal framework under POSH Act
  • Redressal mechanism
  • Case studies and real-life scenarios

Internal Committee (IC) Support

End-to-end support for Internal Committees to ensure effective handling of complaints in line with legal guidelines.

  • Formation of IC as per law
  • Capacity building for members
  • Inquiry process and documentation
  • Report preparation

Gender Sensitization

Create awareness about gender biases and promote respectful and inclusive behaviour in workplaces.

  • Understanding gender stereotypes
  • Identifying unconscious bias
  • Respectful communication
  • Workplace inclusivity practices

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI)

Build inclusive environments where diversity is respected and every individual feels valued.

  • DEI fundamentals
  • Inclusive leadership
  • Bias identification and reduction
  • Equitable workplace practices

Policy & Compliance Support

Assist organizations in developing and implementing policies aligned with legal and ethical standards.

  • POSH policy drafting
  • Review of existing policies
  • Code of conduct
  • Compliance review

POCSO Awareness Programs

Focus on child safety, protection, and legal awareness for institutions and communities.

  • Understanding POCSO Act
  • Identifying signs of abuse
  • Roles of institutions and caregivers
  • Reporting mechanisms
